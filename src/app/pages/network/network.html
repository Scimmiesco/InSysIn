Connection States
ESTABLISHED Active — data is flowing between your computer and the server. This is what you want to see when browsing, streaming, or video calling.
LISTEN Server mode — this app is waiting for incoming connections on a port. Your web server (nginx), SSH daemon, or Spotify showing local control.
SYN_SENT Trying to connect — your computer sent a request and is waiting for a reply. If it stays here, the server is down, blocked by a firewall, or too slow.
SYN_RECEIVED Handshake — the server received your connection request and is finalizing the setup. Brief state, rarely seen.
CLOSE_WAIT Remote side disconnected — the other end closed the connection but your app hasn't closed it yet. Usually temporary.
TIME_WAIT Just closed — the connection ended and the system is waiting briefly to catch any delayed final packets. Normal, fades in seconds.
FIN_WAIT_1 Started closing — your computer sent the shutdown request to the other end.
FIN_WAIT_2 Acknowledged — the remote end agreed to close. Waiting for its final goodbye packet.
CLOSING / LAST_ACK / CLOSED Finished — connection is terminated or in the final milliseconds of cleanup.
